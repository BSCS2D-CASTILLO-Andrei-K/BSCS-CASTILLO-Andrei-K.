MY SKILLS

LOGICAL THINKING
- Logical thinking uses reasoning skills to objectively study any problem,
which helps make a rational conclusion about how to proceed.

FLEXIBILTY
- The capacity to adjust to short-term change , quickly and calmly,
so that you can deal with unexpected problems or tasks effectively.

TIME MANAGEMENT
- Time management is being able to effectively complete tasks in the time
that someone has to work. Is is important because it helps to increase productivity
and decreases stress and anxiety from procrastinating.
